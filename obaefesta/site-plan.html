Overview

Purpose

And when that special moment comes and it's necessary to celebrate? A good decoration can make this
moment even more special. Whether it's a birthday party for someone who brightens your life, a wedding,
or any other event. We are there to help people make the environment much more special with a creative
decoration made especially for them and make those moments unforgettable. For this, our services include
table and chair rental, decoration, and ornamentation service.

Audience

This site is intended for people interested in knowing the service provided by "Oba é Festa", such as
knowing the details of these services, products offered, hiring, or scheduling the decoration of an event.
Some of these products offered, which can be found on display on the website, are table and chair rentals,
as well as decoration items and also contact for scheduling a decoration service with a focus on birthday
parties, with themes for both adults and children.

About Us

Oba! Now it`s time to introduce who we are to you. And to the beginning, we want to introduce who is behind
the Oba É Festa. That is Maria da Conceição, a warrior woman. She has three children which she has to create
by herself as a single mother. And she works a lot to get this objective. Her greater professional
experience is as a cooker for many years in restaurants in the city. Also, she worked as a housekeeper in
her youth. And the opportunities were low, she gives a way seeling snacks on a bike. So, she understands
serving, worrying, and taking care of others. As a cooker, she learned about giving amazing experiences to
people. Unfortunately, because of health problems, she has to abandon her profession as a cooker, but the
desire to serve and to enchant never died. And then borns the Oba.
Every day is a special day. And everybody has reasons to celebrate. A new child was born, a birthday for
someone special, or who knows a wedding. Even if you want to do a big or a little party, celebrating is
essential. By celebrating we can show our gratitude and we can share our happiness with those who care about us.
Celebrating, making parties, and hanging out with others have the power to make us more mentally healthy.
Celebrating has the power to liberate endorphin, serotonin, dopamine, and oxytocin, which are hormones that
make us feel good and happier. So, don't celebrate it is a loss of opportunity to spoon all these benefits.
Celebrating also has the power to make us more confident, alert, and cheerful.
We don't want people sad. We want to be part of your reasons for being happy. The Oba is here to help you
have these benefits. So, let us go celebrate.
