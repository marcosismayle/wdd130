About Us

Oba! Now it`s time to introduce who we are to you. And to the beginning, we want to introduce who is behind the Oba É Festa. That is Maria da Conceição, a warrior woman. She has three children which she has to create by herself as a single mother. And she works a lot to get this objective. Her greater professional experience is as a cooker for many years in restaurants in the city. Also, she worked as a housekeeper in her youth. And the opportunities were low, she gives a way seeling snacks on a bike. So, she understands serving, worrying, and taking care of others. As a cooker, she learned about giving amazing experiences to people. Unfortunately, because of health problems, she has to abandon her profession as a cooker, but the desire to serve and to enchant never died. And then borns the Oba. Every day is a special day. And everybody has reasons to celebrate. A new child was born, a birthday for someone special, or who knows a wedding. Even if you want to do a big or a little party, celebrating is essential. By celebrating we can show our gratitude and we can share our happiness with those who care about us. Celebrating, making parties, and hanging out with others have the power to make us more mentally healthy. Celebrating has the power to liberate endorphin, serotonin, dopamine, and oxytocin, which are hormones that make us feel good and happier. So, don't celebrate it is a loss of opportunity to spoon all these benefits. Celebrating also has the power to make us more confident, alert, and cheerful. We don't want people sad. We want to be part of your reasons for being happy. The Oba is here to help you have these benefits. So, let us go celebrate.
